## 2. Phone Numbers



## 8. Password Strength

-   **At least 8 chars, 1 letter, 1 number:**

``` regex
^(?=.*[A-Za-z])(?=.*\d)[A-Za-z\d]{8,}$
```

-   **Stronger (1 uppercase, 1 lowercase, 1 number, 1 special char, min
    8):**

``` regex
^(?=.*[a-z])(?=.*[A-Z])(?=.*\d)(?=.*[@$!%*?&])[A-Za-z\d@$!%*?&]{8,}$
```
